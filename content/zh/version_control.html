Kyuubi 版本控制

Overview

Kyuubi 源代码位于 Apache git 存储库中，可从此处获得： https://gitbox.apache.org/repos/asf?p=incubator-kyuubi.git

更改也反映到 github 存储库： https://github.com/apache/incubator-kyuubi

The Official Global Conference Series of The Apache Software Foundation, connecting open source technology enthusiasts from around the world to share the latest technology developments and practices.

Apache Kyuubi is an effort undergoing Incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), sponsored by the Incubator. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
